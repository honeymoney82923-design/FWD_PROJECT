Sydney, Australia

The Harbour City

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: The Harbour & Opera House

Start your morning at Circular Quay. Take a guided tour of the iconic Sydney Opera House, then walk through the Royal Botanic Gardens to Mrs Macquarie's Chair for the best harbor views.

Day 2: Bondi to Coogee Walk

Head to famous Bondi Beach early. Grab a coffee, then tackle the stunning coastal cliff walk from Bondi to Coogee, passing through Tamarama and Bronte beaches. Enjoy a seaside lunch at the Coogee Pavilion.

Day 3: Taronga Zoo & The Rocks

Take a scenic ferry ride across the harbor to Taronga Zoo to see native Australian wildlife. Return to the city to explore the historic cobblestone streets, pubs, and markets of The Rocks neighborhood.
