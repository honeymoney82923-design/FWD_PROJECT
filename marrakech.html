Marrakech, Morocco

The Red City

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: The Medina & Souks

Dive into the chaotic beauty of the Medina. Wander through the labyrinthine souks, haggle for spices, textiles, and ceramics. End your day at the vibrant Jemaa el-Fnaa square as the food stalls open and performers gather.

Day 2: Palaces & Gardens

Visit the stunning Bahia Palace with its intricate mosaic tiles and peaceful courtyards. In the afternoon, escape the heat in the vibrant blue Jardin Majorelle, famously restored by Yves Saint Laurent.

Day 3: Atlas Mountains Escape

Take a day trip out of the city to the foothills of the Atlas Mountains. Enjoy a traditional mint tea with a Berber family, or challenge yourself with a hike up to the beautiful Ouzoud Waterfalls.

Trip Details

  • Best Time to Visit:
    March to May, or September to November
  • Currency:
    Moroccan Dirham (MAD)
  • Language:
    Arabic, Berber, French
