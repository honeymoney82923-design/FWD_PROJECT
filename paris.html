Paris, France

The City of Light and Love

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Iconic Landmarks

Start your day at the Eiffel Tower. Walk along the Seine to the Arc de Triomphe and stroll down the Champs-Élysées. Enjoy an evening Seine river cruise to see the city illuminated.

Day 2: Art and History

Spend the morning exploring the Louvre Museum. In the afternoon, visit Notre-Dame Cathedral (exterior) and wander the historic streets of Le Marais district.

Day 3: Bohemian Paris

Head to Montmartre, visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica, and watch artists at work in Place du Tertre. Finish your trip with a cabaret show at the Moulin Rouge or a romantic dinner in a traditional bistro.

Trip Details

  • Best Time to Visit:
    April to June, or October to early November
  • Currency:
    Euro (€)
  • Language:
    French, English (widely spoken in tourist areas)
