Cape Town, South Africa

The Mother City

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Table Mountain & City Bowl

Take the aerial cableway up Table Mountain for stunning panoramic views. In the afternoon, explore the colorful streets of the Bo-Kaap neighborhood and stroll through the Kirstenbosch National Botanical Garden.

Day 2: The Cape Peninsula

Drive down the spectacular Chapman's Peak Drive. Visit the Cape of Good Hope nature reserve, and stop at Boulders Beach to see the famous colony of African Penguins up close.

Day 3: Winelands

Take a short drive to Stellenbosch or Franschhoek. Spend the day wine tasting at world-class vineyards, enjoying incredible scenery and gourmet farm-to-table cuisine.
