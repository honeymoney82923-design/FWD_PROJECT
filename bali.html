Bali, Indonesia

The Island of the Gods

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Sunset

Arrive in Denpasar and transfer to your luxury villa in Seminyak. Spend the afternoon relaxing by the pool or at the beach. In the evening, head to Potato Head Beach Club for an unforgettable sunset and dinner.

Day 2: Culture & Rice Terraces

Head to Ubud, the cultural heart of Bali. Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary, explore the famous Tegalalang Rice Terraces, and shop for local crafts at the Ubud Art Market.

Day 3: Island Hopping

Take a fast boat to Nusa Penida. Visit the iconic T-Rex shaped cliff at Kelingking Beach, snorkel with manta rays at Manta Point, and relax on the pristine white sands of Crystal Bay.
