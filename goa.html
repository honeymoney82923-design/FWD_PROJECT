Goa, India

The Pearl of the Orient

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: North Goa Beaches

Start your trip by exploring the famous sandy stretches of Baga, Calangute, and Anjuna. Enjoy water sports during the day and experience the legendary Goan nightlife by the beach in the evening.

Day 2: Historical Old Goa

Travel back in time by visiting the UNESCO World Heritage churches of Old Goa, including the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral. Enjoy a traditional Goan fish curry thali for lunch.

Day 3: South Goa Tranquility

Head to the quieter, pristine beaches of South Goa like Palolem and Agonda. Relax by the sea, try some fresh seafood shacks, and take a sunset boat ride to spot dolphins.

Trip Details

  • Best Time to Visit:
    Mid-November to Mid-February
  • Currency:
    Indian Rupee (INR)
  • Language:
    Konkani, English, Hindi, Marathi
