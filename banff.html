Banff, Canada

Jewel of the Rockies

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Lake Louise & Moraine Lake

Start early at the iconic turquoise waters of Lake Louise. Rent a canoe or hike up to the Lake Agnes Tea House. In the afternoon, visit Moraine Lake (in the Valley of the Ten Peaks) for even more vibrant water colors.

Day 2: Gondola & Hot Springs

Take the Banff Gondola to the summit of Sulphur Mountain for panoramic views of six mountain ranges. Afterward, relax your muscles in the natural thermal waters of the Banff Upper Hot Springs.

Day 3: Icefields Parkway

Drive a section of the spectacular Icefields Parkway towards Jasper. Stop at Peyto Lake and the Columbia Icefield, where you can walk out onto the Athabasca Glacier.
