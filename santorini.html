Santorini, Greece

Jewel of the Aegean

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Oia and Sunsets

Wander through the narrow, winding paths of Oia, admiring the famous blue-domed churches. Find a perfect spot early to watch the world-renowned Santorini sunset over the caldera.

Day 2: Volcanic Beaches

Visit the unique Red Beach and the striking black sands of Kamari Beach. Relax by the warm Aegean waters and enjoy fresh Mediterranean seafood at a local seaside taverna.

Day 3: Sail the Caldera

Take a catamaran cruise through the caldera. Swim in the volcanic hot springs, snorkel in the clear waters, and enjoy a fresh Greek barbecue cooked right on the boat.
