Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Kochi Heritage

Arrive in Fort Kochi. Explore the historic Chinese Fishing Nets, visit the Jewish Synagogue, and the Mattancherry Palace. Enjoy an evening Kathakali dance performance.

Day 2: Munnar Tea Gardens

Drive up to the hill station of Munnar. Visit the sprawling tea estates, the Tea Museum, and enjoy the breathtaking views from Echo Point and Mattupetty Dam.

Day 3: Alleppey Backwaters

Head to Alleppey and board a traditional Kettuvallam (houseboat). Drift through the tranquil backwaters, enjoying freshly prepared Kerala cuisine on board as the sun sets.
