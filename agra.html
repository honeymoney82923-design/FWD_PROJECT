Agra, India

City of the Taj Mahal

Suggested 2-Day Itinerary

Day 1: The Monument of Love

Start your day before dawn to witness the breathtaking sunrise over the magnificent Taj Mahal. Spend hours admiring the intricate marble inlay work. Later, visit the spectacular Agra Fort and enjoy sunset views of the Taj from across the river at Mehtab Bagh.

Day 2: Fatehpur Sikri

Take a scenic drive to the deserted red sandstone city of Fatehpur Sikri. Explore the stunning Buland Darwaza and Jama Masjid. Return to Agra to shop for local marble handicrafts and taste the famous sweet treat, Petha.
