New York City, USA

The City That Never Sleeps

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Midtown Highlights

Start in Times Square, then head to Rockefeller Center. Stroll down Fifth Avenue to Central Park for a relaxing afternoon walk or a carriage ride. End the evening catching a spectacular Broadway show.

Day 2: Downtown & The Statue

Take the Staten Island Ferry for great views of the Statue of Liberty. Walk through the Financial District, visit the 9/11 Memorial, and cross the iconic Brooklyn Bridge at sunset for stunning skyline photos.

Day 3: Art & High Line

Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art or the MoMA. In the afternoon, walk the High Line, an elevated park built on historic freight rail lines, and explore the trendy boutiques and food markets of Chelsea.
